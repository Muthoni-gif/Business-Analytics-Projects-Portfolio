Key Findings

What the Data Reveals

Four business-critical insights emerged across the three dashboard pages.

🏆 Top Region
West

The West region leads in both total sales and profit. California alone accounts for nearly $0.5M in revenue — more than some entire regions combined.

⚠ Profit Leak
Tables

Tables sub-category shows negative profit despite significant sales volume — the company is losing money on every table sold, likely due to excessive discounting.

📈 Best Category
Tech

Technology is the most profitable category with Copiers and Phones driving the highest margins — strong case for increased technology investment.

📅 Seasonal Peak
Q4

Sales spike dramatically in Q4 (Oct–Dec). November and December consistently outperform all other months — critical for inventory and staffing planning.

💡 My Insights From This Project

What I Learned & What the Data Told Me

1

Key finding: "I wanted to give a sales leadership team one dashboard to answer: where are we winning, where are we losing money, and what is driving performance?"

2

What surprised me: "I built 5 DAX measures — Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Total Orders and Avg Order Value — then designed 3 pages for different audiences: executives, regional managers and product teams."

3

Business implication: "The Tables sub-category is generating negative profit — the company is losing money on every sale. That is a pricing or discount policy problem that needs immediate attention."

4

What I would do next: "Connect this to live sales data via a database, add a YoY growth DAX measure, and build an alert that flags when any sub-category margin drops below 5%."
