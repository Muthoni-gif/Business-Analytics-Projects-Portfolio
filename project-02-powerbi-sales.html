Key Findings

What the Data Reveals

Four business-critical insights emerged across the three dashboard pages — each with a clear implication for sales strategy.

🏆 Top Region
West

The West region leads in both total sales and profit — California alone accounts for nearly $0.5M in revenue, more than entire regions combined.

⚠ Profit Leak
Tables

Tables sub-category shows negative profit despite significant sales volume — the company is losing money on every table sold, likely due to excessive discounting.

📈 Best Category
Tech

Technology is the most profitable category with Copiers and Phones driving the highest margins — strong case for increasing technology product focus.

📅 Seasonal Peak
Q4

Sales spike dramatically in Q4 (Oct–Dec) — the monthly trend chart shows November and December consistently outperform all other months by 40%+.

💬 How to Present This in an Interview

The Story Behind the Numbers

1

The business question: "I wanted to give a sales leadership team a single dashboard to answer: where are we winning, where are we losing money, and what's driving performance?"

2

The technical approach: "I built 5 DAX measures — Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Total Orders and Avg Order Value — then designed 3 pages for different audiences: executives, regional managers and product teams."

3

The key finding: "The Tables sub-category is generating negative profit — the company is actually losing money on every sale. That's a pricing or discount policy problem that needs immediate attention."

4

What I'd do next: "Connect this to live sales data via a database, add a YoY growth DAX measure, and build an alert that flags when any sub-category margin drops below 5%."
